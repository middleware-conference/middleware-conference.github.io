Banquet

Middleware 2019 Banquet would take place at the historic California State Railroad Museum, which is at the heart of beautiful Old Sacramento.


California State Railroad Museum provides finest examples of engineering marvels that once connected California to rest of the states in USA. The museum displays 21 lavishly restored locomotives and cars, some of which date back to 1862. There is a full-scale diorama of an 1860s construction site high in the Sierra Nevada as well as a bridge elevated 24 feet above the museum floor.


The railroad museum also provides limited excursion rides at cheap prices for the people to explore and experience the Wild West country-side. Further, the museum lies at the vicinity of Old Sacramento Town, which has kept its decor and design protected in this fast changing era.
